Burger

  1. Create your burger sauce by combining:
    • 2 onions, finely diced
    • 2 tbsp unsalted butter
    • 2 dill pickles, finely diced
    • 3/4 cup mayonnaise
    • 1/2 cup ketchup
    • 1/3 cup chives
    • 2 tbsp worcestershire sauce
    • Salt and pepper to taste
  3. In a pan over medium heat, add butter and toast your burger buns until crispy and golden brown
  4. Create your burger balls by rolling your ground beef into your preferred sizes
  5. Sear sliced onions over medium-high heat
  6. Remove onions, add burger balls over medium-high heat (on the higher side though) and flatten them with a weight or spatula
  7. Sear burgers for around 1 minute and 20 seconds, or your preferred internal temperature
  8. Flip your burgers and add a slice of your preferred cheese on top
  9. While the burgers are cooking, cover your burger buns with your sauce
  10. Transfer your patty onto the sauced buns, adding your onions on top
  11. Finish with the top bun and enjoy

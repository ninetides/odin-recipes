Avocado Toast

How to Make Avocado Toast

  1. Grab two pieces of your preferred bread
  2. Butter both sides of said pieces of bread
  3. Toast both sides of bread in a pan until golden and firm
  4. Remove the pit from your avocado, and then use a big spoon to scoop the flesh
  5. Put the flesh into a bowl and mash it up with a fork until your preferred consistency
  6. Mix in a pinch of salt
  7. Spread avocado on top of your toast
  8. OPTIONAL: Scramble some eggs and bacon and layer both on top of the avocado
  9. Enjoy!
